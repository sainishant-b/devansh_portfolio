# ProactiveCare AI

ProactiveCare AI is a full-stack healthcare MVP that helps users track vitals + symptoms and get ML-based, explainable risk insights. It supports health entry CRUD, NLP symptom extraction (DistilBERT when available; TF-IDF + keyword fallback otherwise), top-3 condition prediction with confidence scores, SHAP-first explainability (with a fallback), and a history/trends dashboard.  
> Disclaimer: Not medical advice. Always consult licensed clinicians.
